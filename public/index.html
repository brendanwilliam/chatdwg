About the Project

ChatDWG provides a unique opportunity for learners to engage with the cutting-edge technology of ChatGPT, which has been trained on a vast corpus of human knowledge. By exploring the capabilities and limitations of this technology, learners can gain a deeper appreciation for the potential of natural language processing and its role in shaping the future of communication.

In summary, ChatDWG is a valuable educational resource for anyone seeking to understand and integrate artificial intelligence technology into their lives. Its focus on natural language processing and interactive learning provides a powerful tool for promoting digital literacy and preparing learners for the challenges and opportunities of the 21st century.

Key Features

Spot the Bot

  • Educational game where users guess if text is written by a human or ChatGPT
  • Designed to teach what ChatGPT writing looks like
  • Curated prompts and real ChatGPT generated responses
  • Score to keep track of the user's guessing ability

Educational Resources

  • Directory of educational resources to learn more about ChatGPT
